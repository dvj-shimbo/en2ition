— How en2uition Analysis is Done

En2uition analyzes relationships and marriages based on how the two partners or spouses participate in the relationship or marriage. The participations are analyzed using the triangular theory of love. According to this theory the relationship or marriage is analyzed based on three love-components: passion, intimacy and commitment.
